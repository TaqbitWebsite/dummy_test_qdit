Our Story

Qdit Labs began with a late‑night phone call between two friends—a quantum physicist and a systems engineer—staring at the same problem from different angles: if quantum computers arrive sooner than expected, who will protect the world’s data in between?

In 2022, we set out to answer that question. What started as a research initiative in a small lab has grown into a focused effort to bring quantum‑safe security to the organizations that need it most—defense, banking, telecom, and critical infrastructure.

Our early prototypes validated two truths: hardware‑based Quantum Key Distribution offers unparalleled confidentiality for critical links, and software‑based Post‑Quantum Cryptography is the fastest way for enterprises to start protecting data today. We decided to build both—practical systems that integrate with real networks, not slides.

We’re inspired by customers who move fast but refuse to compromise on trust. That’s why our roadmap is simple: make quantum security deployable, observable, and economically sensible. In the next five years, our goal is to help hundreds of organizations complete their transition to quantum‑safe systems—without downtime and without surprises.

Qdit Labs exists so that long‑lived data stays private, national infrastructure remains resilient, and innovation doesn’t have to wait for perfect timing. We’re building for the moment when “quantum‑safe” just means “secure.”
